# Onboarding Q&A

When adding a new app, collect:

1. **What problem does it solve?**
2. **Who is it for?**
3. **What's new / different?**
4. **How does it make money?** (paid / subscription / freemium / IAP)
5. **Primary geography?** (e.g. UK first)

These feed content generation and store copy templates.

## Generated outputs

- X posts
- Facebook posts
- Instagram captions
- LinkedIn posts
- Reddit-friendly post suggestions
- Blog articles
- Press release drafts
- App Store update notes
- Email newsletter content
- Launch checklist
- Hashtags and content calendar

All drafts require human review before publishing.
